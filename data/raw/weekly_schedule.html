Mobility (Phase phase3): Sun Salutation B (Full Vinyasa), Karandavasana Prep, Hanumanasana Progression

Lunch

Evening

Pre-Bed

Mobility (Phase phase3): Advanced Forward Folds, Advanced Hip Opening
